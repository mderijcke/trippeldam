Make request

Passport and ID

As resident of municipality Trippeldam you can request a passport or ID at the Town Hall of Trippeldam. For that, you don’t have to make an appointment. The central public counter is opened on Monday, Tuesday and Wednesday from 9 am till 5 pm. If you don’t want to wait in line, you can make an appointment by clicking on the ‘Make an appointment’ page.


Marriage

A lot of couples choose to marry in Trippeldam. Do you want to marry in historical Trippeldam or another special location in our municipality? You are more than welcome!


Build and cultivate

Are you going to build, cultivate or demolish? First of all, look at the spatial plans of Trippeldam. These plans determine how you can use your ground and plots and how you have to build. Furthermore, it’s not the first thing you think of, but it’s always neat to inform your neighbors about your build plans and to have a conversation with them, if necessary. Only that way you can reach your goal where everyone is happy about.
